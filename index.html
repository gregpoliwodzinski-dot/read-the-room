Message decoder

Paste any message — text, email, DM, Slack, dating app. Get the likely emotional tone, hidden meaning, and what (if anything) you should do next.

Reply coach

How it works

Simple as three steps.

01

Paste the message

Drop in any message — a text, an email, a dating app opener, a passive-aggressive Slack. No need to explain the whole backstory.

"All good 🙂" — from someone who is clearly not all good.
02

Choose what you need

Decode the meaning, read the tone, spot red flags, or get a reply. Add optional context — who it's from, how well you know them — for sharper results.

03

Get instant clarity

A calm, clear reading — no jargon, no drama. What this likely means, what the emotional tone is, and exactly what to do next.

RTR: "The smile emoji softens what's likely mild frustration or hurt. This probably isn't fully resolved. Worth a gentle check-in rather than taking it at face value."
